Make numerical variable categorical¶

Sometimes, a predictor has numerical value, but you believe the magitute does not have any information. In this case, you should make it a categorical variable. For instance, in Auto dataset, Origin means the origin of car (1. American, 2. European, 3. Japanese).

In [23]:
Auto.origin.unique()
Out[23]:
array([1, 3, 2])

In this case, these numerical value is meaningless. In this case, regression on this predictor could be misleading.
