Be careful, when you change the value of one array, it might affect another array.

In [30]:
x=np.array([1,2,3])
y=x
print(x)
print(y)
x[1]=4
print(x)
print(y)

[1 2 3]
[1 2 3]
[1 4 3]
[1 4 3]

This is because y becomes a view of x. To avoid this, you can do the followings.

In [31]:
x=np.array([1,2,3])
y=x.copy()
print(x)
print(y)
x[1]=4
print(x)
print(y)

[1 2 3]
[1 2 3]
[1 4 3]
[1 2 3]
